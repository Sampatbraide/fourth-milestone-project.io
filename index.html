How it Works

Erkoe is designed to solve problem with user experience at the forefront of every details of its design.

Signup to setup artist profiles

Artist, promoters and labels can create and manage their account. Multiple login and roles can be created within an account.

Artist can create contents

Artist can create, categorise and uploads contents to their fans, promoters and labels. Contents can be restricted by the artist.

Consume published content

Artists, promoters, labels and fans can browse music by genre, trendiness, instruments and region. They can listen and do more.

Interact with members

Artists, promoters, fans and labels can show their impression by following/interacting with them by liking, commenting and messaging.

Third-party integration

Artists, promoters, labels and fans can integrate their social media accounts and gain more popularity by engaging with their fans.

Manage contents easily

Artists, promoters, labels and fans can centralise and manage their contents easily. The contents are securely stored on the web server.
